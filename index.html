01 — About

Who I Am

Hi! I'm Princess Gwenn — a third-year Computer Science student at De La Salle University – Dasmariñas, majoring in Intelligent Systems. I thrive at the intersection of software engineering, machine learning, and thoughtful design.

My work spans deepfake detection models, automata simulators, café directory apps, and advocacy platforms. I'm a consistent First Honors Dean's Lister and an OWWA EDSP I Scholar. Outside of code, I organize academic events as a Campaign Committee Member at the DLSU-D CS Program Council.

I actively attend tech conferences and hackathons, and I'm currently open to OJT opportunities in software development or AI/ML.
